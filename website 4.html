What is Underground?

To be the most efficient venue, both in terms of satisfaction and profit, in Montreal.We started Underground because we believe we can reunite a large community that loves rap and parties in a secret location. We want to offer a safe, enjoyable, affordable experience. We also believe that we can change the experience of clubbing because, for the past few years, we have seen that people didn't like to go out anymore. They just stayed at home watching movies or playing video games, so we decided to create something different. We wanted to bring back the feeling of going out without having to be in a crowded place with loud music; this is why we created our own venue!

The Underground Clubhouse was founded by The Pokus Brothers: three young entrepreneurs who love to party. We've been clubbing in Montreal since way before we could legally get into clubs, and we want to share our unique experience with you in an intimate setting that feels like home. For now, we'll be hosting events at various venues around the city, but our ultimate goal is to open our own nightclub in Vaudreuil.

Please remember to book your ticket if you want to secure your place for the event.
